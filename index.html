Value added services

At Flash Digital Bank, we put our customers at the centre of everything we do. That’s why we focus on providing products and services that are built help to make life easier.

ATM Cards

Access 99.9% of ATM machines island-wide using your Flash debit card to withdraw cash or check your balance. Tap to Pay where accepted and because it’s VISA, your Flash debit card will be accepted at virtually every merchant locally or overseas.

A Digital Bank, Built to Last

Don’t let our youthful good looks fool you - An initiative backed by over 100 years of Banking excellence and trust - the largest private Bank in the island - Flash is designed to be the next evolution in digital financial services and financial wellness for everyone. All Flash accounts sit within Commerical Bank of Ceylon PLC, so your money is 100% safe.

Security

We are committed to protecting your money and your privacy. Our security measures are among the best in the industry, and we are constantly working to improve them. Flash is 100% compliant with CBSL security standards and the data is protected with the highest level of security. All transactions use two-factor authorization and standard secure banking practices.

Access

All digital bank accounts created using the Flash application, have an actual physical bank account at Commercial Bank PLC accessible from any of their 268 branches.

Branchless Banking

Experience 100% end-to-end digital banking – Register, open accounts, start fixed deposits, invest and transact totally digitally.
